Dribbling Drills (and mistakes)

March 2024

Mastering Ball Control

Mastering ball control and dribbling skills. Analyzing common mistakes and how to improve.

Basic Dribbling Techniques

Essential skills for good dribbling:

  • Close ball control
  • Body positioning
  • Head up awareness
  • Quick feet movement

Common Mistakes

Mistakes I've made and how to fix them:

  • Looking down at the ball
  • Too much space between feet and ball
  • Poor body balance
  • Predictable movements

Drill Exercises

My favorite dribbling drills:

  • Cone weaving
  • Figure-8 patterns
  • Speed dribbling
  • Change of direction drills

Tips for Improvement

What I've learned:

  • Practice with both feet
  • Keep your head up
  • Use small touches
  • Work on quick direction changes
