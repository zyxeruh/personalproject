My Dribbling & Control Training Routine

Documenting my dribbling and ball control training routine and progress. Focus on technical skills, ball mastery, and game intelligence.

Warm-up Routine

Before starting any training session, I make sure to properly warm up:

  • 5-10 minutes of light jogging with the ball
  • Dynamic stretching focusing on lower body
  • Basic ball touches and control exercises

Technical Training

My technical training focuses on fundamental skills:

  • Close ball control
  • Dribbling through cones
  • First touch control
  • Ball shielding techniques

Game Situation Practice

To improve my in-game performance, I include:

  • 1v1 dribbling scenarios
  • Pressure control exercises
  • Direction change drills

Recovery

Proper recovery is essential:

  • Cool-down stretching
  • Rest days between intense sessions
  • Proper nutrition and hydration
