My Gym Training Routine

Documenting my gym training routine and progress. Focus on strength, endurance, and overall fitness.

Warm-up Routine

Before starting any workout, I make sure to properly warm up:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio
  • Dynamic stretching
  • Joint mobility exercises

Strength Training

My strength training focuses on compound movements:

  • Squats
  • Deadlifts
  • Bench press
  • Overhead press

Endurance Work

To improve my football performance, I include:

  • HIIT sessions
  • Circuit training
  • Interval running

Recovery

Proper recovery is essential:

  • Stretching after workouts
  • Rest days between intense sessions
  • Proper nutrition and hydration
