My Heading Training Routine

Documenting my heading training routine and progress. Focus on technique, timing, and safety.

Warm-up Routine

Before starting any training session, I make sure to properly warm up:

  • 5-10 minutes of light jogging
  • Neck mobility exercises
  • Upper body stretching

Technical Training

My technical training focuses on fundamental heading skills:

  • Proper heading technique
  • Timing and jumping
  • Ball contact point
  • Directional control

Game Situation Practice

To improve my in-game performance, I include:

  • Defensive heading
  • Attacking headers
  • Jumping and timing drills

Safety Tips

Important safety considerations for heading:

  • Proper neck strengthening
  • Correct technique to avoid injury
  • Age-appropriate training
  • Rest and recovery
