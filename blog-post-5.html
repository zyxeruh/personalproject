Developing proper heading techniques. Safety tips and common mistakes to avoid.

Basic Heading Techniques

Key elements of proper heading:

  • Correct head position
  • Body positioning
  • Timing the jump
  • Neck strength

Common Mistakes

Mistakes I've made and how to fix them:

  • Closing eyes before contact
  • Poor timing
  • Weak neck muscles
  • Incorrect head contact point

Safety First

Important safety considerations:

  • Proper warm-up
  • Neck strengthening exercises
  • Gradual progression
  • Using appropriate ball pressure
