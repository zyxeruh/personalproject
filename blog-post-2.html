My Passing Training Routine

Documenting my passing training routine and progress. Focus on accuracy, technique, and game intelligence.

Warm-up Routine

Before starting any training session, I make sure to properly warm up:

  • 5-10 minutes of light jogging with the ball
  • Dynamic stretching focusing on lower body
  • Basic passing against a wall

Technical Training

My technical training focuses on fundamental passing skills:

  • Short passing accuracy
  • Long passing technique
  • First-time passing
  • Weight of pass control

Game Situation Practice

To improve my in-game performance, I include:

  • Passing under pressure
  • Passing in movement
  • Passing combinations

Common Mistakes

Key mistakes to avoid in passing:

  • Poor body positioning
  • Incorrect follow-through
  • Not checking surroundings
  • Inconsistent weight of pass
