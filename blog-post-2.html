Improving Passing Accuracy

Learning and improving passing techniques. Common mistakes to avoid and tips for better accuracy.

Basic Passing Techniques

Key elements of a good pass:

  • Proper body positioning
  • Correct foot placement
  • Follow-through motion
  • Eye contact with target

Common Mistakes

Mistakes I've made and how to fix them:

  • Not looking up before passing
  • Poor body balance
  • Incorrect foot placement
  • Lack of follow-through

Drill Exercises

My favorite passing drills:

  • Wall passing practice
  • Triangle passing drill
  • Long-range passing
  • One-touch passing

Tips for Improvement

What I've learned:

  • Practice with both feet
  • Focus on accuracy before power
  • Record and analyze your technique
  • Get feedback from coaches
