My Control Training Routine

Documenting my ball control training routine and progress. Focus on first touch, ball mastery, and spatial awareness.

Warm-up Routine

Before starting any training session, I make sure to properly warm up:

  • 5-10 minutes of light jogging with the ball
  • Dynamic stretching focusing on lower body
  • Basic ball touches and control exercises

Technical Training

My technical training focuses on fundamental control skills:

  • First touch control
  • Ball cushioning
  • Directional control
  • Body positioning

Game Situation Practice

To improve my in-game performance, I include:

  • Control under pressure
  • Control in movement
  • Control with different body parts

Common Mistakes

Key mistakes to avoid in ball control:

  • Poor body positioning
  • Not cushioning the ball
  • Looking down at the ball
  • Rigid body movement
