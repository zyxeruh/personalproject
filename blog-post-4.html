Perfecting Shooting Techniques

Perfecting shooting techniques and accuracy. Common errors and how to fix them.

Basic Shooting Techniques

Key elements of a good shot:

  • Proper body positioning
  • Plant foot placement
  • Follow-through motion
  • Ball contact point

Common Mistakes

Mistakes I've made and how to fix them:

  • Poor body balance
  • Incorrect plant foot position
  • Not following through
  • Wrong contact point on the ball

Drill Exercises

My favorite shooting drills:

  • Static shooting practice
  • Moving target shooting
  • First-time shooting
  • Power and accuracy drills

Tips for Improvement

What I've learned:

  • Practice with both feet
  • Focus on technique before power
  • Work on different shooting angles
  • Record and analyze your shots
